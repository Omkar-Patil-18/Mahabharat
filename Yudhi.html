Yudhishtira

In the Hindu History of Mahabharata, Yudhishtira (Sanskrit: युधिष्ठिर) is the eldest son of King Pandu and Queen Kunti and the king of Indraprastha and later of Hastinapura (Kuru). He was the leader of the successful Pandava side in the Kurukshetra War. At the end of the epic, he was the only one among his brothers to ascend to heaven with his mortal body. Later, he had to experience a hell for his half-lie spoken during the 15th day of the war. He was also blessed with the spiritual vision of second sight by a celestial Rishi as a boon.
